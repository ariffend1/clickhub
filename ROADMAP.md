# ClickHub Roadmap

Future features and planned improvements for ClickHub.

## 🎯 Vision

ClickHub aims to be the **all-in-one IT Operations Management platform** combining task management, support ticketing, asset tracking, and team collaboration.

## 📊 Current Status

**Version**: 1.3.2 (Stable)
**Release Date**: June 2026
**Active Development**: Yes ✅
